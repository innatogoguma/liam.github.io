My Family

Liam Family

Here is a picture of my family, specifically me on the left, my father in the middle, and my mother on the right. Pardon for the picture because we don’t have any recent pictures together, it was taken in 2018, and my mother edited it, HAHA! Anyway, I want to give appreciation to my hardworking father. He was in South Korea for 13 or 14 consecutive years just to provide for the needs and wants of our family, especially for my education. So, when he came home, I didn’t interact with him that much because I was shy. I grew up seeing my mother only face to face. But now, I am slowly adapting to the environment, and I can talk with him only if there are important concerns.


Moreover, I also want to give appreciation to my loving mother. The woman of my life, she sacrificed everything just for me. When I was young, she was very strict, she would not allow me to go out without her. But now, she is letting me do what I want but considering limitations. Whatever choices I make, she supports me wholeheartedly—whether it's my academic decisions or personal ambitions. She never pressures me to be the best in school, yet every time I achieve something, she never fails to show how proud she is.
